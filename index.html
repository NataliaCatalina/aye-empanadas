About Empanadas

Empanadas are a very versatile dish. These delicious bundles can be prepared with so many different types of fillings, from traditional savoury to mouthwatering sweet fillings. They are made by folding a sheet of dough over the ingredients and sealing before cooking. The name comes from the Spanish word empanar, which means “to roll or cover.”
